🎮 How to Play

Goal: Survive endless waves of monsters, earn XP, level up, and unlock new weapons.

Controls:
Desktop: WASD to move · Click to attack
Mobile: Left joystick to move · Right side to aim & attack

Tips:
• Defeat monsters to earn XP and level up
• New weapons unlock as you level up
• Press Q or use the hotbar (1/2) to switch weapons
• Watch out for bosses at the end of waves
• Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded!
